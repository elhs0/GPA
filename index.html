How the math works

Quality points, not letter grades, drive the result.

The formula

Every course contributes quality points: its grade point multiplied by its credit hours. A 4-credit course graded A contributes far more to your GPA than a 1-credit course graded A — and this ledger weights it that way.

GPA = Σ(grade point × credits) / Σ(credits)

Worked example

Course Cr. GP QP
Programming44.016.0
Database33.39.9
Mathematics23.77.4
Total933.3

33.3 ÷ 9 = 3.70 GPA
